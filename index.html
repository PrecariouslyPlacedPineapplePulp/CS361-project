About

"Data Wizard" is a program made by David Li for CS 361 in July 2023.

To use the program, select any number of statistics that interest you in the top middle panel, then in the bottom middle panel, select a csv file from your computer with the "choose file" button, pick what type of data is in your file, and a color scheme. Lastly, click the "Apply" button to see your results.

You can return to the customization screen after clicking the "Apply" button by clicking the "return" button that will appear afterwards.
